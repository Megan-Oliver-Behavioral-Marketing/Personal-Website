Community / Nonprofit
Rebuilding Engagement Through Decision-Focused Communication
Problem
Consistent activity, inconsistent participation. Communication wasn't designed to guide people toward action at the moment they encountered the message.
Behavioral Insight
The breakdown wasn't awareness — it was at the decision level. No structured reason to act in the moment of encounter.
Strategy
Redesigned the decision environment: unified weekly narrative, channel-specific roles, trigger timing, friction reduction, and system-based execution.
Outcomes · 3 months
+125% organic social engagement · +20% first-time participation · +23% livestream viewership
